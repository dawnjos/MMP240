Description

BuddyBed is a top-of-the-line weather resistant dog bed that combines the best PVC polyester canvas material available with a unique eco-friendly styrofoam bead filling - whose contoured oval shape guarantees your dog’s weight will be distributed evenly. No more rolling around trying to get comfortable!

Specifications

  • Furry top that zips off for easy washing!
  • Oval shape for maximum comfort!
  • Removable, machine washable cover made from resilient PVC polyester canvas.
  • Recycled, eco-friendly styrofoam beads for maximum comfort.
  • Water and weather-resistant.

Pick-up the Handset!

1. Remove the handset from the cradle.
2. Place the handset to your ear and listen for a dial tone.
3. The dial tone should be a mid-ranged, constant pitch.
4. If you do not hear a dial tone, something is wrong.
5. Step two.

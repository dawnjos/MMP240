Say Hello - Hang Up

1. Listen for 'HELLO'.
2. You can now talk, say 'HELLO' back.
3. Have a conversation.
4. Say 'GOODBYE'.
5. Return the phone to the cradle when you are finished with your conversation.
6. THE END... You Did It.

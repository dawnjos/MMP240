Step 2

Dial the Number

1. Place your finger on the first number you wish to dial.
2. Each number has a corresponding hole that will allow you to place your finger in to begin dialing that number.
3. Rotate the dial clockwise until your finger touches the metal stop.
4. Remove your finger from the opening. This will allow the dial to return to its original position.
5. Find your second number and repeat steps until you enter all the remaining numbers you are calling.
6. Step three.

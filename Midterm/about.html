About HIPSTAR

HIPSTAR is a US magazine launched in 2018 under the Conde Nast publication to HIPSTAR, targets the future generation. Like Vogue and many magazine under Conde Nast publication it includes stories about fashion but in HIPSTAR we take it up a few levels and also focus on the current events of our country and the world. The magazine has cut back on its print distribution in favor of online content, which has grown significantly. The magazine has also expanded its focus from fashion and beauty to include politics and current affairs. This magazine offers a voice to the young who have an opinion and need to voice it. That is why we feature young people who has an impact on society and striving for change. HIPSTAR is mostly an online entertainment and news magazine with one purpose “our future generation.”
